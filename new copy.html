Our Heritage & Legacy

History & Heritage

Founded in 1870, Oak Grove School stands as one of the oldest and most esteemed residential schools in the Himalayan region. With over 150 years of tradition, it has cultivated generations of leaders, thinkers, and responsible citizens. Our sprawling campus, surrounded by lush greenery and mountain vistas, provides an environment conducive to holistic development.

Our mission is to blend rich traditions with modern educational practices, emphasizing academic excellence, character building, and social responsibility.

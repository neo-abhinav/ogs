About Our School

Discover our rich heritage and commitment to educational excellence

A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Established on June 1, 1888, Oak Grove School has been a cornerstone of quality education in the picturesque hills of Mussoorie for over 135 years. Founded by the British Raj for the children of employees of the East Indian Railway, our institution has evolved into one of India's premier residential schools.

Spread across 250 acres of lush greenery at an altitude of 2,025 meters, our campus provides an ideal setting for learning and personal growth. The school follows the educational philosophy of Jiddu Krishnamurti, emphasizing holistic development and self-discovery.

We take pride in our rich heritage while embracing innovation in education. Our student-teacher ratio of 10:1 ensures personalized attention, and our comprehensive curriculum balances academic rigor with creative and physical development.

Our Academics

Comprehensive educational programs designed for intellectual growth

Junior School (Classes I-V)

Our Junior School program focuses on foundational learning in a nurturing environment. We emphasize:

  • Literacy and numeracy skills development
  • Inquiry-based learning approaches
  • Social and emotional development
  • Creative expression through arts and crafts
  • Introduction to environmental awareness

With small class sizes and specialized teachers, we ensure each child receives individual attention to build strong academic foundations while fostering curiosity and love for learning.

Middle School (Classes VI-VIII)

The Middle School years are crucial for intellectual and personal development. Our program features:

  • Subject-specialized teaching across disciplines
  • Development of critical thinking skills
  • Introduction to research methodologies
  • Leadership and teamwork opportunities
  • Enhanced focus on technology integration

We help students navigate this transitional phase with academic support and guidance counseling, preparing them for the challenges of senior school.

Senior School (Classes IX-XII)

Our Senior School prepares students for board examinations and higher education with:

  • CBSE and IGCSE curriculum options
  • Specialization in Science, Commerce or Humanities
  • University and career counseling
  • Advanced research projects
  • Competitive exam preparation

With a 95% board exam pass rate and excellent university placement record, our senior program equips students with the knowledge and skills for future success.
